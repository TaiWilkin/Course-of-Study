Course of Study

Seedlings Level: Spring Start

Ages 4-7

This level begins at the very beginning of education, for children who can't yet read or add. However, for older children, the majority of subjects are appropriate for students up the end of first grade. Simply supplement with a phonics program and/or mathematics course at the appropriate level where the content is too easy.

Holidays in the Seedlings Level

This is a fully secular curriculum. Lessons are included for secular US holidays - these are removed from the ordered lessons to allow flexibility in scheduling, and can be inserted where needed. For example, if you are on Day 16 on Memorial Day (May 25th), complete the Memorial Day lesson that day, and continue with Day 16 on May 26th.

It’s also an important part of Social Studies for the children to study the history and beliefs behind their family’s particular holidays and traditions. As such, the curriculum includes slightly fewer than the average number of weekdays per month, to allow families to take a day off for their particular holidays. On those days, “lessons” should be participation in your family’s holiday traditions and some simple talks about the history and beliefs of those traditions.

For non-religious families, consider using the day off to focus on something that strongly reflects the values and traditions of your family.
