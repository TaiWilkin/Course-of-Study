Interactive Spelling Websites

These are free, phonics-based spelling websites for children to learn to spell via phonic analysis of words. These will be found especially beneficial for older children who struggle with spelling after being taught via the memorization method.

  • Phonic Spelling This program is inspired by the Primary Speller by Edwin S. Richards. It currently contains 60 lessons, representing two years of work for younger learners. An older learner using the program for remediation can complete up to a lesson a day.
  • McGuffey Spelling This program is inspired by the McGuffey Readers (with some adaptations of content for secularization and modernization). It currently only contains 10 lessons. The primary difference between this course and the Phonic Spelling program is that this course includes dictation lessons.

Phonics: Reading, Spelling and Penmanship

Grades: Kindergarten, First, and Second

This is a free, phonics-based course. Each lesson includes instruction in reading, spelling, and penmanship.

  • Cursive/Script First (recommended) Children learn to read in print (manuscript) and script (cursive), and to write in script.
  • Print/Manuscript Only Children learn to read and write in print (manuscript) only.

Geography and Science through Nature Study

Grades: Kindergarten, First, Second

This is a free course in home geography, science and nature study. This is a multi-year course; aim to complete one lesson per week. Adapted from the work of C. C. Long.

  • Home Geography for Primary Grades

Prang's Primary Course in Art Education

Grades: Kindergarten, First

This is a free art course focusing on hands-on activities with shapes, forms, colors and patterns, following a guided progression of skills. Students will work with modelling, drawing, cutting, paper-folding, and design-laying.

  • Year I: Simplified The lesson content in this edition and the expanded edition is nearly identical. However, the simplified edition has shorter, more readable directions for a more open-and-go experience.
  • Year I: Expanded The lesson content in this edition and the simplified edition is nearly identical. However, the expanded edition has longer, more detailed directions in the first nine weeks of lessons, for those who want extra support. Plan to read the directions before the lesson.

Handwriting
