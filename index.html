Reading

Phonics: Reading, Spelling and Penmanship

Grades: Kindergarten, First, and Second

This is a free, phonics-based course. Each lesson includes instruction in reading, spelling, and penmanship.

  • Cursive/Script First (recommended) Children learn to read in print (manuscript) and script (cursive), and to write in script.
  • Print/Manuscript Only Children learnt to read and write in print (manuscript) only.

Art

Prang's Primary Course in Art Education, Year I

Grades: Kindergarten, First

This is a free art course focusing on hands-on activities with shapes, forms, colors and patterns, following a guided progression of skills. Students will work with modelling, drawing, cutting, paper-folding, and design-laying. Ideas for correlating art lessons to other subjects are included.
